Tableaux Tableaux

Tableaux Tableaux is an interactive web-based application that proposes collage-making as a tool for citation. Using the pictorial tableau as an aesthetic framework, it offers an immersive digital experience that situates the user in mid-century Black urban domestic and community spaces and allows them to arrange, drag, and layer images to create collages that evoke considerations of place-making, memory, and belonging. Drawing from the Chicago Department of Urban Renewal archives, Tableaux Tableaux subverts depictions of displacement by memorializing the spaces that once existed. Users engage chance operations to load background images of the intimate domestic and community spaces juxtaposed with foreground elements like found print media and ceramic tile textures, offering a dynamic platform for storytelling and cultural reflection.

Tableaux Tableaux invites you to engage with these ideas through your own compositions, blending historical reflection with personal creativity. As you arrange, drag, and layer images, consider the interplay of structure and freedom, history and innovation, as it unfolds in your own tableaux.
